Welcome to the World of Nausicaä

Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind is a classic anime film directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli. It tells the story of Nausicaä, a young princess who fights to protect her kingdom and its inhabitants from a destructive force known as the Toxic Jungle.

About the Film

Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind was released in 1984 and has since become a beloved classic of Japanese anime. The film features stunning animation, a compelling story, and memorable characters, making it a must-see for fans of the genre.
